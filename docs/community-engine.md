# PCGH Community Engine

## Overview

The PCGH Community Engine manages member communities and creator circles.

The purpose of the Community Engine is to:

* Organize users
* Support amplification
* Support creator collaboration
* Enable community growth
* Maintain healthy distribution patterns

---

# Member Community Philosophy

Members belong to communities.

Members do not participate in:

* Task systems
* Reward systems
* Engagement exchanges

Members participate in:

* Discovery
* Community
* Amplification

---

# Community Rules

* Maximum 100 members per community
* Unlimited communities
* Automatic assignment
* Automatic community creation
* Community visibility restrictions
* Community performance tracking

---

# Community Assignment

Example:

```text
User #1
        ↓
M001

User #101
        ↓
M002

User #201
        ↓
M003
```

---

# Community Visibility

Members see:

```text
Their community only
```

Administrators see:

```text
All communities
```

---

# Community Performance

Communities are measured by:

* Participation
* Consistency
* Authenticity
* Amplification
* Community health

---

# Community Growth

When a community reaches:

```text
100 members
```

PCGH automatically creates:

```text
Next available community
```

Example:

```text
M001 → 100 members
M002 → 100 members
M003 → created automatically
```
