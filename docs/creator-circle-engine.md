# PCGH Creator Circle Engine

## Overview

The Creator Circle Engine organizes creators into structured creator communities.

Creator circles support:

* Creator organization
* Creator analytics
* Creator recognition
* Creator governance
* Platform scalability

---

# Creator Circle Rules

* Maximum 100 creators per circle
* Unlimited creator circles
* Automatic creator assignment
* Automatic creator circle creation
* Creator circle analytics
* Creator circle recognition

---

# Creator Assignment

Example:

```text
Creator #1
         ↓
C001

Creator #101
         ↓
C002

Creator #201
         ↓
C003
```

---

# Creator Visibility

Creators may view:

* Their creator circle
* Their standing
* Their badges

Administrators may view:

* All creator circles
* All creator analytics
* All creator reputation
* All creator recognition

---

# Creator Circle Analytics

Measured by:

* Creator participation
* Campaign quality
* Community amplification
* Creator consistency
* Creator reputation
