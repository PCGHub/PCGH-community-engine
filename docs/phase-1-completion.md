# Executive Summary

Phase 1 of the PCGH Community Engine focused on designing the complete business architecture, relational architecture, operating model, governance model, and platform intelligence systems before implementation.

The objective of Phase 1 was to answer one question:

> What exactly is PCGH?

Phase 1 successfully established the complete foundation of the PCGH operating system.

---

# Platform Positioning

PCGH is NOT:

* A follow-for-follow platform
* A like-for-like platform
* An engagement pod
* An engagement farm
* A reward-based engagement network
* A task marketplace

PCGH IS:

> A community-powered creator discovery and audience amplification operating system.

Creators purchase visibility and distribution.

Communities participate in discovery and amplification.
