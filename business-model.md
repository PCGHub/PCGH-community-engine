# PCGH Business Model

PCGH uses a creator credit system.

Creators purchase credits to distribute their content through community networks.

Example:

* Purchase 1,000 credits
* Submit 2 links daily
* Each link costs 100 credits
* Daily spend = 200 credits

Community members:

* Do not earn money
* Do not earn credits
* Do not compete for rewards
* Participate as part of creator communities

Revenue sources:

* Credit purchases
* Creator subscriptions
* Premium creator tools
* Analytics services
* AI creator services
* Community amplification campaigns
